This is a final project for IPH 312. Using Billboard's Top 100 charts for the week of November 3, 2018 for pop songs and K-Pop songs, chord information for the top 25 songs on each list were gathered from sources such as Ultimate Guitar and Chordify. The data obtained from this extration and analysis is displayed on this website.

The graphs on this site were generated using Chart.js and Google Charts.
